ICMLA 2026 · Main Track · Rochester Hills, MI, USA
A novel framework for detecting adversarial training attacks in federated learning systems through analysis of optimization trajectory geometry and spectral dynamics. By monitoring low-rank subspace evolution and gradient manifold perturbations across training rounds, the system identifies malicious participants without accessing raw client data.
